Dice Game

Rules

  • When you click the ROLL DICE! button, you will roll up to 10 dice, one at a time.
  • If you roll a 1, the game stops and all further rolls are forfeited.
  • If you roll a 2, 3, or 4, you win nothing but get to roll again.
  • If you roll a 5, you get 5 gold coins and you get to roll again.
  • If you roll a 6, you get 6 gold coins and you get to roll again.
